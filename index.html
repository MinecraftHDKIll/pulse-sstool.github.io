Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does my AntiVirus flag Pulse for the virus?

    AntiVirus might flag Pulse for virus because we are sending data to our discord server for generating unique password.

  • I found an error, where can I report it?

    You can report errors in Pulse itself (Error Report section) or you can join our discord server for help.

  • Where do I get my username and password for login?

    You will find your username and password at our Discord server in #login channel.

  • Is everything really free?

    Yes! Everything in Pulse is free and we don't have any plans for adding paid features.

  • I don't know how to use the program...

    Do not worry! You can right click on any module and read info about it. If you don't know how to login, read #how-to-use on our discord server!
